About Leo & Lena

A Partnership Built on Coffee & Cameras

We met five years ago in a small café in Zurich. Between sipping cappuccinos and discussing the merits of analog vs. digital, we realized we shared a singular obsession: collecting fleeting moments.

What started as weekend nature walks with our trusty old cameras quickly blossomed into "Lens & Love". We don't just take pictures; we aim to capture the feeling of the day. The way the light hit your dress, the tear your father wiped away, the wild energy on the dancefloor.

We believe the best photos happen when you forget we're even there.

What to expect from us:

  • Documentary Style We capture events as they unfold naturally.
  • Pre-Wedding Consultation Coffee dates to align our vision with yours.
  • Fast Delivery Teasers within 48h, full gallery in 4 weeks.
